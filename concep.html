Conceptual Design

  • NextGen AgriTech aims to revolutionize modern agriculture through advanced technologies and sustainable practices.

  • The project includes a centralized control system, sensor networks, and high-speed connectivity for real-time monitoring

  • Precision farming techniques like GIS and VRT will be employed for optimal resource allocation and crop management.

  • Drones and satellites equipped with sensors will provide valuable data on crop health and field conditions

  • Automation and robotics will be introduced for tasks such as seeding, harvesting, and weed control, reducing manual labor and improving efficiency.

  • Smart irrigation systems will be implemented to minimize water wastage and ensure efficient water usage

  • Artificial intelligence algorithms will analyze data to monitor crop health, predict disease outbreaks
