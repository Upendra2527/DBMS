Selection

Selection (σ): The selection operation selects rows from a table that satisfy
a specified condition or predicate. It is denoted by the σ symbol.
Example: Let's say we have a table called "FARMER" with columns: "FNAME",
"AGE" and "CROPS." To select all FNAME with an age greater than 15, we
can use the selection operation as follows:

σ(age > 15)(FARMER)
